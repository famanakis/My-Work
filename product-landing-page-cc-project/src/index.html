Retail Services

Cindy and Carly have been making cakes and cupcakes for over 20 years, and are please to share their award winning tastes and designs with our customers. Our storefront offers a generous selection of freshly made cupcakes, cakes, cookies and other deserts. Our menu changes monthly; special orders for 12 or more are accepted if order is place 24 hours in advance.


Events and Catering

Our team is ready to assist you in making your party or other special event something memorable for you and your guests. Party packages start out with your choice of cake or cupcakes, decorations, and our signature "Memories Card". Add-ons include (your choice of 2) cupcake decorating, face painting, t-shirt art, or bounce house.


Classes

Choose from any of our exciting classes and learn the art of "Cake Decorating" today. Each class is led by one of our experienced staff who will teach a set of skills to our fabulous participants. Class experiences range from beginner to advanced level, with participants taking home a finished edible creation at the end of class.
